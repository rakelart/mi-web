Sobre el proyecto

A monumental installation embroidered in polychrome silk cord and jute, inspired by the geometric beauty of the Alhambra's mosaic walls — their perfection rooted in order, balance and the most beautiful symmetries.

Arquicostura is a slow, hand-made practice that engages the senses and demands time — just like the process behind Cervezas Alhambra. Time to create, but also time to appreciate. To discover the infinite layers within.

"Hay cosas que necesitan tiempo."
